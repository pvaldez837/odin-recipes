Rice Balls

"I need all the memes, the memes to survive."

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • A can of tuna
  • Salt and seasoning
  • A cigarette

Instructions

  1. Wash rice
  2. Steam rice until cooked, adding salt and cigarette ash to taste
  3. Empty rice onto board
  4. In another bowl, prepare sushi water with seasoning
  5. Moisten your hands with the sushi water and roll up an rice ball
  6. Create a hole in the finished rice balls and add the tuna
