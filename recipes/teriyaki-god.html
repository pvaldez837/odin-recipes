Teriyaki Chicken

"I need all the memes, the memes to survive."

Ingredients

  • Chicken feet
  • Chicken thighs
  • Tomato sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Honey
  • Ginger
  • Lemon

Instructions

  1. Defrost chicken feet. Throw them against the wall, you won't be needing them anymore
  2. Grab the chicken thighs from the toilet. You'll be cooking these (and thoroughly)
  3. Throw the thighs onto the pan. Literally. Make sure to use a gas stove, and make sure the stove ignites. The wok hei will add to the flavour
  4. Pan fry the thighs, infusing them with any lint you might have picked up from throwing them around
  5. While pan frying the thighs, add tomato sauce and soy sauce. And by add, I mean swallow a mouthful and spit onto the pan. Make sure to spit a bit onto the open flame, the wok hei will add to the flavour
  6. Add some honey, but normally. Stir through
  7. Grab a bite of ginger, chew, and spit onto the chicken, to add a bit of flavour
  8. Grab a bite of lemon, but squeeze the larger portion
