Toilet Sriracha Shrimp

"I need all the memes, the memes to survive."

Ingredients

  • Shrimp, ideally toilet shrimp
  • Garlic
  • Adobo seasoning
  • Sriracha
  • Unsalted butter
  • Chilli flakes
  • Jasmine rice

Instructions

Rice

  1. Wash jasmine rice
  2. Add washed jasmine rice and water to a pot
  3. Steam rice until cooked

Sriracha shrimp

  1. Grab the shrimp from the potty.
  2. Add oil and chopped garlic to a pan. Heat and saute
  3. Add shrimp and saute
  4. Add butter and baste shrimp. Saute
  5. Add adobo seasoning and chilli flakes. Saute
  6. Once shrimp is almost cooked, add sriracha to shrimp. Saute until caramelized
  7. Serve shrimp with rice
